Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account to use Tabmangment?

Yes, you need to sign in with an account to use Tabmangment. This allows us to sync your tabs and settings across all your devices seamlessly. Creating an account is quick and free!

How does the Free plan work?

The Free plan gives you access to basic tab management for up to 10 tabs. You can view, organize, and manage your tabs with ease. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited tabs, custom timers, and advanced features!

Does it sync across devices?

Yes! Your tabs, settings, and subscription status automatically sync across all devices where you're signed in. Switch between computers seamlessly without losing your workflow.

What happens if I cancel my Pro subscription?

If you cancel your Pro subscription, you'll be automatically downgraded to the Free plan. You'll retain access to your first 10 tabs and basic features. You can resubscribe anytime to regain Pro features!

How do I save important tabs?

Use the "Bookmark All" feature (Pro only) to save all your important tabs with one click. This creates bookmarks of your tabs so you can safely close them and reopen anytime. Perfect for research sessions or important work you want to preserve!

Can I sign out and use a different account?

Absolutely! Click the logout button in the extension to sign out completely. You can then sign in with a different account or create a new one. Each account keeps its own separate data and subscription status.

What data do you collect?

We only collect your email for authentication and basic tab data to provide sync functionality. We never access your browsing history or personal information. Your privacy is our priority.
