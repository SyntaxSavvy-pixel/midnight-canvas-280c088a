Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we protect your data and respect your trust.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, sell, or share any personal information. Your privacy is our top priority.

We believe in complete transparency. Tabmangment operates entirely on your device with zero external data transmission.

Local Storage

All your settings, preferences, and tab management data are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API.

This data never leaves your computer and is never transmitted to external servers. You have full control over your information.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions to provide its functionality:

Tabs: To manage, organize, and close tabs based on your preferences.

Storage: To save your settings and timer configurations locally on your device.

These permissions are used solely for the intended purpose of tab management and nothing else.

Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit any information to third-party companies, advertisers, or data brokers.

Your browsing habits, tab data, and usage patterns remain completely private and are never accessible to anyone but you.

User Authentication

If you choose to use premium features, authentication is handled securely through Stripe and Firebase.

We only store the minimal information necessary to verify your subscription status. Payment details are handled entirely by Stripe and never stored by us.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us through the extension's built-in contact feature.
